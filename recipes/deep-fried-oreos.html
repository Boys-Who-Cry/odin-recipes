Deep Fried Icecream

A delicious cookie desert!

!

A better way to enjoy our favorite creme filled cookie.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 quarts Vegetable Oil
  • 2 teaspoons Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup Pancake Mix
  • Oreos

Steps

  1. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375 deg Farenheit
  2. Whisk together in a bowl, the egg, milk, and 2 teaspoons of vegetable oil until smooth. Then stir in the pancake mix.
  3. Dip cookies into the batter.
  4. Place into hot frying oil.
  5. Fry a few at a time to avoid crowding the deep fryer.
  6. Fry cookies until they are golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  7. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
