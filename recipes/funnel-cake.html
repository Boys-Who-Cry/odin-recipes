Funnel Cake

A delicious treat!

A great desert after a long day at the carnival.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart Oil
  • 1 1/2 cups of Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 tspn Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 3/4 cup of Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oil to 375 deg Farenheit on skillet
  2. Beat milk and eggs together in a bowl.
  3. Combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt, then stir into egg mixture.
  4. Grab a funnel, and using your finger to plug the hole, pour in some batter, then let the batter pour onto skillet.
  5. Fry until golden brown, 1-2 minutes per side, drain on paper towels.
  6. Sprinkle on powdered sugar.
