Deep Fried Icecream

A delicious icecream treat!

An fun yet unconventional way to enjoy icecream.

Ingredients

  • Icecream
  • Corn Flakes
  • Vanilla Cookies (like Golden Oreos)
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Combine crushed Corn Flakes, crushed cookies, and sugar into a large bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs into a bowl.
  3. Take a scoop of icecream and place it into the eggs.
  4. After dipped into the eggs, roll around in the cornflake mixture.
  5. Repeat the two previous steps.
  6. Place into freezer for 3 hours or even overnight.
  7. Fill a pot with oil, over medium heat, until it reaches 375 deg Farenheit
  8. Fry icecream in small batches, and either serve immediately or place back into freezer until ready to serve.
